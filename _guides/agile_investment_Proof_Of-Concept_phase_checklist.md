During the PoC Phase:

* Agile Scrum team is assigned to implement and test solution recommendation
* Provide and Measure outcome of PoC team
* Review PoC recommendation 
* Business / Technical review to approve/reject progressing to next Phase


*Input Checklist Before Starting PoC*

** Scope and Approach
ğ„‚ PoC scope is defined
ğ„‚ High-level business needs have been identified prioritized
ğ„‚ High-level technical needs and requirements have been identified prioritized
ğ„‚ Approach for PoC phase had been defined


** People and Resources
ğ„‚ Sponsoring Department/Executive has been defined
ğ„‚ Key personnel resources and skill sets identified and secured
ğ„‚ Assigned Product Owner from the Business Team
	ğ„‚ Assigned Scrum Master
	ğ„‚ Agile coaching support and guidance 
ğ„‚ Team members are fully dedicated to the PoC effort

** Development Environment
ğ„‚ Has development environment been defined? (e.g. test/dev environment, production, etc.)
ğ„‚ Has required access been identified, requested and setup completed?
