Connecting to an InSite Live Broadcast

Gather together

To save bandwidth, watch the Town Hall together with your colleagues in a meeting room

1. Make sure you're using the right connection:

  • In a GSA office? Don't use WiFi. Connect your laptop to a docking station or a network cable
  • In a field office with low bandwidth? Connect your laptop to your cell phone hotspot and turn on VPN
  • Teleworking? Connect your laptop to your cell phone hotspot and turn on VPN
  • On a Mac? Use the Horizon desktop (VDI) and follow the directions for VDI below

2. Click the link below based on where you are watching the video. Select "Save" if prompted.
