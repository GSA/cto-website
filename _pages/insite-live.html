InSite Live: Follow these steps to connect to your broadcast

Gather together

To save bandwidth, watch the Town Hall together with your colleagues in a meeting room

1. Make sure you're using the right connection:

  • In a GSA Office? Connect your laptop to a docking station or a network cable.
  • In a field office with low bandwidth? Connect your laptop to your cell phone hotspot and turn on VPN
  • Teleworking? Connect your laptop to your cell phone hotspot and turn on VPN
  • On a Mac? Use the Horizon desktop (VDI) and follow the directions for VDI below.

2. Click the link below based on where you are watching the video:

  • I am watching from a Horizon desktop (VDI) in any location
  • I am teleworking and watching on VPN
  • I am watching from a field office with low bandwidth using my cell phone hotspot
  • I am watching from a field office connected to my docking station
  • I am watching from a Regional Office Building
  • I am watching from an office in Washington, D.C.

3. If you are prompted to save, select Save.
